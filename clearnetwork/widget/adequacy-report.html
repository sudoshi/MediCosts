About adequacy scoring: Scores are computed from provider counts in insurer Machine-Readable Files per 45 CFR §147.211. Adequacy thresholds reflect CMS guidance (≥50 PCPs → full PCP score, ≥200 specialists → full specialist score, ≥20 facilities → full facility score). Scores do not constitute CMS network adequacy certification. Always verify with your insurer before receiving care.
