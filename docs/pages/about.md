# About & Data Sources (`/about`)

**Component:** `client/src/views/AboutView.jsx`
**Nav group:** *(bottom utility group)*

## Purpose
Transparency page explaining MediCosts' data sources, methodology, legal basis for disclosure, and project attribution.

## Sections
- Mission statement
- Data source registry (10 federal datasets with record counts, update frequency, legal basis)
- Methodology notes (how charges, payments, and quality scores are calculated)
- Limitations and disclaimers
- Legal: HIPAA non-applicability, 45 CFR §180 hospital charge disclosure mandate

## Data Sources Listed
1. CMS Medicare Inpatient Charges (DRG-level, 2023)
2. CMS Hospital Quality Initiative (HCAHPS, safety, readmissions, mortality, stars)
3. CMS Open Payments (Sunshine Act — 30M+ payments)
4. HCRIS Hospital Cost Reports (FY2023–2024)
5. NPPES NPI Registry (2.7M active providers)
6. CMS Part D Drug Spending (5-year, 2019–2023)
7. CMS Post-Acute Care Provider Compare
8. HRSA HPSA Shortage Designations (88K areas)
9. CDC PLACES (32,520 ZIP codes, 26 health measures)
10. ClearNetwork (2.1M provider-network links)
